Hook Slice

Description

Master the art of the hook and slice in this thrilling action game! Use your agility and combat skills to defeat enemies, explore vibrant worlds, and uncover hidden secrets. Swing, slice, and conquer in Hook Slice!

Instructions

PC Controls:
• Movement: WASD or Arrow Keys
• Attack (Slice): Left Mouse Button
• Hook: Right Mouse Button
• Jump: Spacebar
• Interact/Use: E Key
• Special Ability/Skill: Q, R, F (or other designated keys)
• Crouch/Sneak: C Key (if applicable)
• Inventory/Menu: I or Tab Key
• Pause: Escape Key or P
